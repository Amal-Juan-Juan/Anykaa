ANYKAA

Our Passion. Your Beauty.

Anykaa is an Indian cosmetics company, founded by Anna Jai Joseph in 2022 and headquartered in D-726 LH, VIT Vellore.

It sells beauty, wellness and fashion products across different websites. In 2022, it became the first Indian unicorn startup headed by a woman in her hostel

The startup was also hyped up by Anna's roomates, Vinita Vaswini and Madusmita Mukherjee

    The brand specialises in facilitating an extensive collection of
  • cosmetics
  • skincare
  • haircare
  • fragrances
  • bath and body
  • luxury items
  • wellness products for both women and men

Anykaa also hosts beauty and fashion content via Anykaa TV, its YouTube channel. It contains informational videos about beauty, cosmetics, and styling.

    Some of Anykaa's notable campaigns include
  1. #BreakTheHashtag (with Tapsee Pannu)
  2. #WhatMakesYourBeautiful
  3. Beauty in Her Story
  4. Rakshak (a Raksha Bandhan special film)
